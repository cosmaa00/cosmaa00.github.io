About Ella

Ella Cosma discovered pickleball and immediately fell in love with the sport's unique blend of strategy, athleticism, and community. What started as a casual hobby quickly transformed into a competitive pursuit as her natural talent became evident.

Known for her fierce determination and sportsmanship, Ella brings energy and excitement to every match. She is committed to growing the sport and inspiring the next generation of players.

When not on the court, Ella enjoys connecting with fans, training to improve her game, and working with youth programs to introduce pickleball to new players.
